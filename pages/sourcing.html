Our Philosophy

The culinary and sourcing teams at FreshBites use a rigorous process to choose responsible ingredient suppliers with similar values. Thanks in part to their support, our meals produce a 31% lower life-cycle carbon footprint than those of the same size from a supermarket.

Products

Fair and fresh from the ocean

The Better Fish® offers the world’s first Fair Trade-certified ocean-farmed white fish. This supplier’s focus on quality and flavor has made barramundi the fastest-growing fish species in the country. Thanks to climate-resilient practices that minimize food waste and environmental impact, they earned the U.S. Secretary of State’s 2021 Award for Corporate Excellence in Climate Innovation.

Produce straight from the source

Wonderful Citrus is on a mission to provide the safest, freshest-quality citrus for today and tomorrow. This company diverts its surplus material to create other products like juice and pulp—40,000 tons!—for human consumption. Going even further, Wonderful Citrus aims to divert 100% of its waste from landfills in California, Texas, and New Jersey by 2024.

Oil and vinegar powered by the sun

Colavita’s bottles aren’t the only thing about them that’s green! This Italian goods company—known for its olive oil and vinegar—participates in the Net Carbon Zero program for all imports with their partner Kuehne+Nagel, the largest worldwide freight forwarder. Additionally, Colavita uses solar power for all its U.S. and Italy facilities.

Sustainability’s part of the package

Pratt is the world's largest, privately-held 100% recycled paper and America's 5th largest corrugated packaging company. Dedicated to sustainability and environmental innovation, Pratt works with communities and businesses to recover old corrugated containers and mixed paper products, diverting them from landfills and repurposing them into high-quality packaging solutions.
