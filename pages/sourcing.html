Sourcing

at FreshBites

Our Philosophy

The culinary and sourcing teams at FreshBites use a rigorous process to choose responsible ingredient suppliers with similar values. Thanks in part to their support, our meals produce a 31% lower life-cycle carbon footprint than those of the same size from a supermarket.

Products

Fair and fresh from the ocean

FreshBites offers the world’s first Fair Trade-certified ocean-farmed white fish. Our focus on quality and flavor has made barramundi the fastest-growing fish species in the country. Thanks to climate-resilient practices that minimize food waste and environmental impact, We Earned the 2021 Award for Corporate Excellence in Climate Innovation.

Produce straight from the source

FreshBites is on a mission to provide the safest, freshest-quality citrus for today and tomorrow. This company diverts its surplus material to create other products like juice and pulp—40,000 tons!—for human consumption. Going even further, FreshBites aims to divert 100% of its waste from landfills in Egypt by 2024.

Oil and vinegar powered by the sun

FreshBites' bottles aren’t the only thing about them that’s green! This Egyptian company—known for its olive oil and vinegar—participates in the Net Carbon Zero program for all imports with their partner Kuehne+Nagel, the largest worldwide freight forwarder. Additionally, FreshBites uses solar power for all its facilities in Egypt.

Sustainability’s part of the package

Pratt is the world's largest, privately-held 100% recycled paper and Egypt's 5th largest corrugated packaging company. Dedicated to sustainability and environmental innovation, Pratt works with communities and businesses to recover old corrugated containers and mixed paper products, diverting them from landfills and repurposing them into high-quality packaging solutions.
